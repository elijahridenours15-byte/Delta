Service Bay

Core Systems Pass

Run the same order every time: power, cooling, fuel-air, braking, steering, driveline, then tires and recovery gear. The goal is repeatable field checks before parts swapping.

ElectricalBattery health, terminal condition, grounds, charging output, fuse isolation, and starter draw symptoms.
CoolingCoolant level, hose integrity, fan behavior, leak points, temperature spikes, and pressure loss pattern.
Fuel + AirFilter service interval, injector cleanliness, intake obstruction, vacuum leaks, and pump/no-start separation.
Brake + SteeringFluid condition, pad wear, line seepage, rotor heat signature, steering play, and rack response.

Quick Loadout

  • Socket roll, pliers set, trim tool, breaker bar
  • Coolant-safe funnel, nitrile gloves, shop towels
  • Recovery strap, shackles, inflator, patch kit

Pre-Trip Check

  • Battery voltage and terminal tightness
  • Fluid scan: oil, coolant, brake, washer
  • Tire pressure, tread, and spare readiness
  • Lights, wipers, charging accessories, jack

Post-Fault Workflow

  • Capture symptoms before disconnecting anything
  • Separate electrical failure from fuel-air failure
  • Confirm heat, leak, smell, and vibration sources
  • Only replace parts after a narrowed diagnosis
