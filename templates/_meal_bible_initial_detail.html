Skillet Stack: Chicken Thigh with Broccoli Florets and Jasmine Rice

Chicken Thigh, Jasmine Rice, Broccoli Florets, and Garlic Herb Butter configured as a perishable field meal build.

P-001 1 serving 18-22 min 12-inch skillet + lid Breakfast High Protein Low Sodium
Operational Brief

Cold-chain meal. Stage chilled ingredients last and keep raw proteins separated until the pan is hot.

Hold above 140F for service or chill under 40F within 2 hours; reheat leftovers to 165F.

Prep Plan
Step 1

Stage 10 oz / 284 g chicken thigh, 2 cups cooked jasmine rice, 2 cups broccoli florets, 2 tbsp garlic herb butter, plus clean water, salt, and the listed cook kit.

Step 2

Pat dry, season, and cook until the center reaches 165F. Sear the chilled protein first so the pan develops color and the protein reaches its safe finish.

Step 3

Steam or reheat until hot and loosen with a fork before service. Bring the base to ready-to-eat temperature before the final combine.

Step 4

Steam or saute until crisp-tender. Fold in the produce during the last three minutes to keep bite and color.

Step 5

Stir in off heat so the butter coats instead of splitting. Kill the heat, add the finishing sauce, and rest two minutes before portioning.

Step 6

Portion the batch into 1 serving. Hold above 140F for service or chill under 40F within 2 hours; reheat leftovers to 165F.
