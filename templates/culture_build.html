👥 Ethnic Groups

Persian ~61% Primary ethnic group; majority in central and southern Iran
Azeri ~16% Turkic-speaking; concentrated in northwest; speak Azerbaijani
Kurdish ~10% Indo-European; northwest and western regions; speak Kurdish

Arab ~2% Southwest region; speak Arabic and Persian

⛪ Social Structure & Religions

Social Structure

  • Family Unit: Extended family oriented; patriarchal traditions
  • Social Hierarchy: Religious clerics hold significant authority
  • Gender Roles: Traditional; women increasingly educated and employed
  • Class System: Secular and religious elites; merchant class; working classes
  • Respect: Age, education, and religious knowledge highly valued

Religious Composition

Shi'a Islam ~90-99% State religion; largest denomination
Sunni Islam ~5-10% Minority; mainly among Kurds and Arabs
Zoroastrianism ~0.1% Ancient Persian religion; small community
Christianity & Judaism ~0.1% Small religious minorities

🎭 Cultural Customs & Traditions

Nowruz Celebrations
Spring renewal festival; families gather for Haft Sin (seven symbolic items); involves house cleaning, new clothes, and family gatherings
Carpet Weaving
Ancient Persian art form; intricate designs with historical and symbolic meanings; highly valued as cultural heritage
Tea & Hospitality
Strong tea culture (chai); offering tea is sign of hospitality; social gatherings revolve around chai
Poetry & Literature
Rich literary tradition; works by Rumi, Hafiz, Omar Khayyam celebrated; poetry readings important cultural activity
Courtyard Culture
Traditional homes built around central courtyards; important for family privacy and social life
Bazaar Trade
Ancient covered markets (bazaars); important for commerce, social interaction, and cultural exchange
